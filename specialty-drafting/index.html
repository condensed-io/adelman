It Has To Be Perfect, And It Has To Be Done Quickly

Mr. Adelman enjoys a reputation as a superb legal writer, and his law offices are retained by law firms and others in the legal community who seek drafting of exceptional quality and nuance. As a former judicial law clerk in the most important federal district court in the country, and a veteran of high-level commercial litigation practice in New York City, Mr. Adelman is accustomed to sophisticated, demanding, time-sensitive projects. He specializes in drafting appellate and trial briefs, as well as dispositive and non-dispositive motions, pleadings, discovery requests and responses, confidentiality agreements, mediation statements, settlement agreements and other writings.

Mr. Adelman’s drafting practice affords law firms the opportunity to avail themselves of the talents of a seasoned, persuasive legal writer, while maintaining flexibility in staffing, and avoiding the expense of providing salary, benefits and office space to a senior attorney.

The rate structure of his drafting practice is designed to permit firms to recapture Mr. Adelman’s fees as a pass-through expense, either on an at-cost basis or with a premium added.
